Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Decription

How could you go wrong? 3 great things combined into one sandwich. Doesnt matter if you're a kid or grown adult. Let's dive into this.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly

Steps

  1. Lather one piece of bread with grape jelly
  2. Rinse the butter knife off, we don't need to do extra dishes here
  3. Smear the other piece of bread with peanut butter
  4. cut the crust off and cut diagonally
