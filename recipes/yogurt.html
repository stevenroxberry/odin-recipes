My Morning Yogurt

Description

Healthy, Delicious, and Nutrious

If you're like me, I like my sleep. I don't wake up extra early to make a big breakfast. This is my morning breakfast routine that will please almost everyone.

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk Yogurt
  • Granola
  • Chopped Walnuts
  • Fresh Fruit (ie blackberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • Honey
  • Pumpkin Seeds

Steps

  1. Put desired amount of yogurt in bowl
  2. Put the rest of tyhe ingredients in and stir
  3. Enjoy!
