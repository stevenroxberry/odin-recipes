Pasta Bolognese

Descriptions

Let me guess, you're at work and have no idea what to make for dinner for 100th day in a row. Well, look for further! Pasta Bolognese, a classic dish that is a simple as it delicious.

It only takes a few ingredients and basic cooking skills to make. without further ado, let's cook!

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Dry Pasta (any kind)
  • 1 Jar Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 LB Ground Beef
  • Basil
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Seasoning

Cooking Steps

  1. cook the pasta
  2. cook ground beef in sauce pan
  3. once ground beef is cook, add tomato sauce
  4. drain pasta once el dente
  5. put sauce on low heat, turn off pasta
  6. add the amount of pasta needed to beef/sauce mixture
